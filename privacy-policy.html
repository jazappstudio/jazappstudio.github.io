Notation Chess Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Notation Chess is an Apple Watch chess app designed to work without collecting your personal data.

Data Collection

Notation Chess does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party data collection services.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores gameplay preferences locally on your device, including your selected color, engine search depth, and move variety setting. This information is not transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Network Access

Notation Chess does not require an account and does not transmit your gameplay data over the internet.

Children's Privacy

Notation Chess does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app's privacy practices change. Updates will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer through the support information listed on the App Store, or email jazappstudio@gmail.com.
